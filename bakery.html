About Us

My Baking Corner is an Indian established Bakery that serves wide variety of items. Founded in 2020 during corona pandemic, the bakery focuses on hygiene, cleanliness and best customer experiences. The items are cooked within the bakery with atmost care and precautions. The workers are sanitized and wear masks and gloves compulsorily during their work. The prices are afforable without any compromise on the taste and quality of the dishes.

We offer our clients a full scope of breads, forte breads, morning merchandise, cakes and baked goods. We have onsite service as well as home delivery with minimum charges that can be applied to delivery. We also take catering and strive best to give you the best.

Spreading bliss and commending each snapshot of life is the mantra at My Baking Corner’. ‘My Baking Corner’ started their endeavor with Handmade Chocolates and gradually moved into Cakes, Pastries and unified items.
